ABOUT

BrainergyAfrica is a community of yound Africans who believe in making an impact in our community.
We found a gap in the educational sector and we aim to bridge this gap by being a chaneel for provision of books in to students in public schools who have no access to it. 

MISSION

To provide Academic and nonacademic books to less priveledged students in nooks and crannes of Africa in order to improve the standard of education and promote the reading culture.

VISION

To be the number one  site for every  student in need of academic materials.
To be the largest online community that caters to the academic needs of less priveledged secondary school students in Africa. 
To make books (academic and personal development) and other academic materials available to students in dine need of it.

BRAINERGYAFRICA IS LOOKING FOR BOOK DONORS.

According to UNESCO (United Nations Educational Scientific and Cultural Organisation) 26% of a country’s budget should go to the education sector but in most African countries, this percentage hasn’t been implemented.This problem of funding has lead to further problems in the education sector in Africa. There is poor infrastructure, lack of high performing student, malpractice to list a few.BrainergyAfrica is rising to the challenge by impacting in the education sector in Africa starting with Nigeria. They are impacting each secondary school (both Junior and Secondary) by donating textbooks, newbooks, motivational books and handouts to less privileged schools.This goal can be achieved when individuals donate for free their used books to these students through us. The process is simple. Register to be a member of the community and start donating your old or used textbooks and you would be touching lives.A confirmation mail will be sent to you and we would get in touch with you right away. In every school, a book head would be elected to have access to the internet and give out books to the students who need them.Start making change by donating your used books today!
